A Precarious Job: Being a Delivery Rider in Southeast Asia

A story about the issues that delivery riders in Singapore, Manila, and Ho Chi Minh face. The format of a comic was chosen to present data gathered from interviews with delivery riders in a warm and sensitive way. Data visualisations were woven throughout to ground the narrative and reveal important insights.

This comic was also an exploration of a relatively new format of data storytelling for Kontinentalist.
